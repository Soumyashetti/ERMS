About us

Genius Aircon Pvt Ltd is an ISO 9001:2015 Certified company, established in year 2008 with the operation and service contracting of chillers

We have set high standards for rendering prompt, quality service by which the customers are benefitted with minimum downtime of the equipment. Our goal is to provide the service better than the best available in the industry.

Our endeavor is to reduce the life cycle cost of HVAC system with-out compromising the comfort levels to the end users.

We are registered as per the statutory obligations of the government. We would carry out service and maintenance to your satisfaction. We also render operations of plant apart from maintenance.
